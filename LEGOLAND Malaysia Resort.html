LEGOLAND Malaysia Resort

Welcome to LEGOLAND Malaysia Resort, a fun-filled destination perfect for families and LEGO enthusiasts. The resort includes a theme park with over 70 rides, slides, shows, and attractions, all designed with LEGO bricks. Enjoy themed areas such as LEGO Kingdoms, Imagination, and LEGO Technic, as well as the interactive Miniland, where famous Asian landmarks are recreated using millions of LEGO bricks.

The resort also features a water park with various slides and wave pools, and a themed hotel with LEGO-themed rooms, ensuring a memorable stay for kids and adults alike.
